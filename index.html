2008-2018

Animal genetics, pig genomics, and population adaptation

Early work centers on porcine miRNA expression, GWAS in pig resource populations, mitogenomes, wild boar domestication, and cold-region adaptation. Public papers list Huazhong Agricultural University agricultural animal genetics affiliations in this arc.

2019-2026

New gene origination and genome evolution

Nature, Genome Biology and Evolution, Genome Research, Science Advances, and Nature Genetics records place major work in de novo gene origination, protein structural evolution, 3D genome architecture, enhancer capture, and human disease phenotypes.

2022-2025

Systems genetics and medical genomics

Recent clinical and translational genetics papers connect the profile with West China Hospital / Sichuan University systems genetics, rare variants, neurodegenerative disorders, and Mendelian disease phenotyping.

2025

MR and GWAS summary-statistics methods

PLOS Genetics and UChicago Knowledge records list Jianhai Chen on int2MR, a Mendelian randomization framework for detecting exposure-by-group interactions using GWAS summary statistics.
